Company Overview

Who We Are

SR Engineering Services (Pvt) Ltd is a full-service construction and engineering company headquartered in Pakistan. We specialize in delivering end-to-end solutions across construction, civil engineering, infrastructure development, MEP services, and solar energy.

Founded by experienced engineering professionals, the company has grown to become a trusted partner for government organizations, private sector companies, real estate developers, and industrial clients across the country.

Our multidisciplinary workforce combines deep technical expertise with modern project management practices, ensuring every project is completed safely, on time, and within budget.
