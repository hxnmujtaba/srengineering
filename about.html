Company Overview

Who We Are

SR Engineering Services (Pvt) Ltd is a full-service construction and engineering company headquartered in Pakistan. We specialize in delivering end-to-end solutions across construction, civil engineering, infrastructure development, MEP services, and solar energy.

Founded by experienced engineering professionals, the company has grown to become a trusted partner for government organizations, private sector companies, real estate developers, and industrial clients across the country.

Our multidisciplinary workforce combines deep technical expertise with modern project management practices, ensuring every project is completed safely, on time, and within budget.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

Key moments that shaped SR Engineering Services into what it is today.

Sep 2022

Company Founded & Registered

SR Engineering Services (Private) Limited incorporated under the Companies Act, 2017 by Engr. Saad Raja, Engr. Raja Israr Kiyani, and Engr. Usayram Sultan. SECP CUIN: 0211186. FBR and PEC registrations completed in the same year.

2022

First Government Contracts Secured

Awarded renovation contracts for NADRA HQ Islamabad and NADRA Bharakahu — first major government projects establishing credibility in the public sector. Also commenced construction of Academic Blocks A & B at Air University Kharian Campus.

2022–2023

DHA Islamabad Partnerships

Commenced major infrastructure works with Defence Housing Authority Islamabad including sewerage, drainage, and civil works across DHA Phase 3 and Phase 5, Islamabad.

2023

Major Infrastructure Milestone

Completed construction of Main Drain (28'×12'), DHA Phase III — contract value Rs. 80.2 Million. Also completed Sports Complex and Landscaping at Air University Kharian Campus.

2024

PEC Licence Upgraded & Ongoing Expansion

PEC Licence No. 29689 (Category C5/E) renewed with validity to June 2025, authorizing works up to Rs. 65 Million. Multiple ongoing projects including water supply, drainage, and filtration plant works across DHA Islamabad.

2025

Present — Growing Stronger

Continuing to expand capabilities, workforce, and geographical reach across Pakistan. Serving government and private sector clients with 18+ completed projects and multiple ongoing works.
